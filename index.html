About

I am Carles Navarro, from L'Ampolla, a little town on the south coast of Catalonia. I recieved my Bachelor's degree in Chemistry from the URV (Universitat Rovira i Virgili) with the final thesis completed following the Erasmus+ programm in the Leuven-Limburg University, Belgium.

During the Bachelor's my plan was to continue my studies pursuing a PhD within a field in which I could combine innovation, science and technology. As by the time I finished the degree I did not find any Master's that convinced me, I decided to took a gap year to clear my mind and do something that had been in my mind for a while, study physics. During the course 2019-2020 I started to study physics at UNED (National University of at Distance Studies) and to read and look for interesting and innovative topics in research. Then, I discovered a field I had never heard about, cheminformatics, which combines chemistry, computer science and information science -for example in the areas of topology, chemical graph theroy, information retrieval and artificial intelligence. This fitted perfectly with what I was looking for, so I decided to enroll the Master in Bioinformatics for the Health Sciences from the UPF (Universitat Pompeu Fabra). And here I stand, working hard to improve my programming skills and looking forward to start researching on the field.

When I am not studying or working (what means never right now) I like to do sport to reliev the stress, mostly calisthenics, running and boxing. For entreteinment I watch NBA, read about science, economy and fantasy (I am a Song of Ice and Fire geek) and when I was young and wild, I used to play video games like Call of Duty and FIFA the most of my time, but nowadays I am almost retired do tue the lack of time.

MacroBuilder

Macro-Complex Reconstructor

MacRe or Macro-Complex Reconstructor is a bioinformatic program which aims to reconstruct a biological macro-complex given a set of predefined paired interactions pdb files of the different chains of the complex. This program is written in Python following a superimposition strategy to build the macro-complexes, using the module Bio.PDB from Biopython as the main tool for performing the reconstruction.
